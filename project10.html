Project 3: Responsive Design for Furniture Booking App

Introducing the Furniture Booking App, a game-changer in the world of interior design and home decor. This app is your one-stop solution to effortlessly finding and booking the perfect furniture pieces to transform your living spaces.
App Highlights:
Seamless Exploration:Browse through an extensive collection of furniture items, from sofas to tables, curated to cater to various styles and preferences.
Visualize Your Space:Utilize augmented reality (AR) technology to virtually place furniture in your room. See how different pieces fit and complement your existing decor before making a decision.
Personalized Recommendations:Receive tailored furniture suggestions based on your interior design preferences. Whether you love modern minimalism or classic elegance, the app has options that match your style.
Easy Booking Process:Booking furniture has never been easier. With a user-friendly interface, you can reserve your chosen items with a few taps and select convenient delivery or pickup options.
Real-time Availability: check the availability of your desired pieces. No more disappointment due to out-of-stock items the app keeps you informed.
Collaborative Wishlist:Create and share a wishlist with family members or roommates. Collaboratively decide on furniture pieces that everyone loves and bring your collective vision to life.
Expert Advice:Access design tips and advice from professionals to help you make informed decisions about furniture choices and arrangements.
Enhance Your Living Space:
The Furniture Booking App brings innovation and convenience to your interior design journey. With its intuitive interface, immersive AR features, and personalized recommendations, transforming your living space has never been this exciting. Experience hassle-free furniture selection and create a home that truly reflects your unique style. Download the app today and unlock a world of design possibilities.
